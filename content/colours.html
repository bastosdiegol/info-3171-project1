DailyTube Colour Palette

This page showcases the core color palette used in the DailyTube project, emphasizing clarity, vibrancy, and readability across various media.

Citrine Orange (#FFBD59)

Energetic and vibrant, ideal for accents.

Primary Yellow (#DCD21A)

Vibrant and bold, used to highlight key areas.

Cream (#E4E6C3)

Soft and warm, adds a subtle layer of warmth.

Dark Brown (#261C15)

Rich and grounding, perfect for text and accents.

Light Beige (#F7F7F2)

Clean and neutral, used for backgrounds and contrast.
