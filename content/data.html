Data Visualization Guidelines

The data visualization style for DailyTube is designed to enhance clarity, engagement, and comprehension. By applying our color palette, typography, and design principles, we create visualizations that are both visually appealing and easy to understand. The following guidelines will help ensure consistent use of visual data across the platform.

Principles

  • Use the color palette to convey meaning and maintain consistency.
  • Ensure all text is legible by using appropriate font sizes and colours.
  • Visualizations should enhance understanding, not distract or overwhelm.
