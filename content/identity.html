Visual Identity

The goal of a cohesive visual identity for DailyTube is to create an instantly recognizable and inviting experience for our users, whether they're on our app, visiting our website, or interacting with our social media channels. Rooted in our brand values — "invite everyone, creativity, and positivity" — our visual identity is crafted to make each interaction feel welcoming, vibrant, and inspiring. This includes a thoughtfully chosen color palette, consistent typography, and visual treatments that embody DailyTube's approachable, creative, and uplifting style.

These Brand and Visual Identity Guidelines are designed to ensure that all of DailyTube's visual assets are used in a way that reflects our commitment to inclusivity and positive engagement. Within these guidelines, you'll find clear instructions on how and when to use each of our visual elements, alongside policies and standards for consistent usage. By following these guidelines, we reinforce DailyTube as a trusted, joyful space for daily video content, connecting people and creativity across all platforms.
