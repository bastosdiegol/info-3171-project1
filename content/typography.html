DailyTube Typography

For apps and websites, use the Google font Dosis.

Dosis a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Pablo Impallari. It features rounded, clean, and friendly letterforms, making it highly suitable for both digital and print media. Originally designed for web and user interface design, Dosis provides excellent legibility at various sizes and across different devices. Its subtle rounded corners give it an inviting, creative look, while its geometric structure maintains professionalism and precision.
